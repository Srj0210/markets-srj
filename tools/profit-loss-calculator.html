How is Stock Profit Calculated?

The stock profit formula is simple: Profit = (Sell Price - Buy Price) × Shares - Commissions. Your return on investment (ROI) is calculated as the profit divided by your total cost, expressed as a percentage.

What About Taxes on Stock Profits?

In the United States, stock profits are subject to capital gains tax. If you held the stock for less than one year, your profit is taxed as short-term capital gains at your ordinary income tax rate. If you held for more than one year, you pay the lower long-term capital gains rate (0%, 15%, or 20% depending on your income).
