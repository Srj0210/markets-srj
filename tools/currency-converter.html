Why International Investors Need Currency Conversion

If you're investing in US stocks from India, UK, or anywhere outside the US, your actual returns are affected by currency exchange rates. A stock might go up 10% in USD, but if your local currency strengthened 5% against the dollar, your real return is only about 5%.
