Understanding Global Markets

Global stock markets operate in different time zones. When the US market closes, Asian markets open. European markets overlap with both US and Asian sessions. This creates a 24-hour cycle of global trading activity.

How to Invest in International Markets

The easiest way to invest in international markets from a US brokerage is through country-specific ETFs: INDA for India, EWU for UK, EWG for Germany, EWJ for Japan. These trade on US exchanges during US market hours.
