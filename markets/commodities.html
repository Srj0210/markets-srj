How to Invest in Commodities

The easiest way to invest in commodities is through ETFs. GLD tracks gold, SLV tracks silver, and USO tracks crude oil. You can buy these just like regular stocks through any brokerage.

Gold is often considered a "safe haven" during market downturns and inflation. Oil prices affect energy stocks and the broader economy.
