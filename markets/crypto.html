What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital currency secured by cryptography. Bitcoin, created in 2009, was the first. Today thousands of "altcoins" exist for decentralized finance, smart contracts, and more. Crypto is extremely volatile — prices can swing 10-20% daily. Most advisors recommend keeping crypto to 5% or less of your portfolio.
