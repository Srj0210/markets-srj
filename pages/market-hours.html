Best Times to Trade

First 30 minutes (9:30–10:00 AM ET): Highest volatility and volume. Gap ups/downs from overnight news get resolved. Best for momentum traders.

Midday (11:30 AM–2:00 PM ET): Lowest volume period. Markets often drift sideways. Not ideal for active trading.

Last hour (3:00–4:00 PM ET): The "power hour." Institutional investors make large trades, volume picks up, and trends often accelerate into the close.
