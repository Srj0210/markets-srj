Why Economic Data Matters for Stocks

Economic reports directly influence Federal Reserve policy decisions, which in turn affect interest rates, stock valuations, and market sentiment. For example, higher-than-expected CPI (inflation) data may cause stocks to sell off as markets price in higher interest rates.

Pro tip: Avoid opening new positions 30 minutes before major economic releases (CPI, Jobs Report, FOMC). Volatility spikes can be extreme.
