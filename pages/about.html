About SRJahir Markets

SRJahir Markets is a free, real-time US stock market tracking platform built and maintained by SRJahir Tech. Our mission is to provide retail investors and market enthusiasts with clean, fast, and reliable market data — completely free.

What We Offer

Live US market indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones, Russell 2000), real-time stock quotes for thousands of US-listed companies, interactive price charts with multiple timeframes, personal watchlist that saves in your browser, sector performance tracking, top gainers and losers, and curated financial news from trusted sources.

Our Technology

SRJahir Markets is built with modern web technologies — vanilla JavaScript for speed, Cloudflare Workers for our API layer, and Finnhub for market data. The entire platform is designed to be lightweight and fast, loading in under 2 seconds on any device.
